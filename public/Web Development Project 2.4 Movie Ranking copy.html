The Best Movies According To Stephen

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

I like this film because it ties the trilogy together really well and has many action-packed scenes.

The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers

I like this film because it has three plotlines that are all going at the same time.

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

I like this film because of the many different places that the characters go.
